How to Use

  • Add a Task: Fill out the form with the task details and click "Add Task".
  • Manage Tasks: Tasks appear in the grid. Use the buttons on each card to mark them as Complete, Edit their details, or Delete them.
  • Persistence: Your tasks are saved automatically in your browser. If you refresh or close the page, they will remain saved!
